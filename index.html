Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 27, 2026

👋 Introduction

Barista Sensin ("we", "our", or "us") is a coffee brewing guide application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

🔒 Information Stored Locally

The following data is stored only on your device and is never transmitted to our servers:

  • Theme preference (dark/light mode)
  • Language preference
  • Brewing history and statistics
  • Favorite recipes
  • Purchased signature recipes
  • Streak data

📊 Third-Party Services

Google AdMob

Our app displays advertisements through Google AdMob. AdMob may collect and process:

  • Device identifiers (Advertising ID)
  • IP address
  • Device information (model, operating system)
  • App usage data
  • Approximate location (based on IP)

Learn more: Google Privacy Policy

Google Analytics for Firebase

We use Firebase Analytics to understand how users interact with our app. Firebase may collect:

  • App usage statistics
  • Device information
  • Crash reports

Learn more: Firebase Privacy

🎯 How We Use Information

  • Provide and improve app functionality
  • Display relevant advertisements
  • Analyze app usage to improve user experience
  • Fix bugs and crashes

🛡 Data Storage & Security

  • All personal preferences and app data are stored locally on your device
  • We do not operate any servers that store your personal data
  • Third-party services handle their data according to their own privacy policies

👶 Children's Privacy

Our app is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

✅ Your Rights

  • Clear all app data by uninstalling or clearing app data in device settings
  • Opt out of personalized advertising in your device's ad settings
  • Reset your advertising identifier in device settings
